Quick Overview

GDAL is a library capable of reading a plethora of raster formats. If you have GDAL installed on your system, check out "gdal-config --formats" to see what formats your installed version supports. If you have a specific format in mind that is supported by GDAL, but not listed under "gdal-config --formats", then you would have to recompile GDAL with support for that format. For more information on GDAL, see http://www.remotesensing.org/gdal/

MEXGDAL is the interface between MATLAB and GDAL. It is capable of reading both the raster metadata and the data itself.

A few m-files are provided for your use, readgdalsimple.m, readgdalband.m, and gdaldump.m. These sit between you and mexgdal, which you probably do not want to call directly.
